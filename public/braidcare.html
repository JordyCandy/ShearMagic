After having your hair braided, it's essential to take proper care of your braids to ensure they stay looking good and to maintain the health of your hair and scalp

Here are a few tips to keep your braids looking good longer:

✨Keep your braids clean✨

Dilute sulfate-free shampoo with water in a spray bottle and apply it to your scalp. Massage it in, and then rinse with lukewarm water. Avoid scrubbing vigorously to prevent frizz.

Consider using a dry shampoo or a scalp cleansing spray between washes to keep your scalp fresh.

✨Hydrate your scalp✨

Apply a lightweight, oil-free scalp moisturizer to keep your scalp from becoming dry and itchy.

Avoid heavy oils and products that can build up on your scalp, as this can lead to product residue and flakiness.

✨Protect your braids at night✨

Keep your braids covered or wrapped while you sleep, preferably in a satin or silk bonnet/scarf to prevent frizz and breakage

✨Avoid excessive pulling and tugging✨

Be gentle when styling and tying your braids. Excessive tension can lead to hair breakage and stress on your scalp.

Use hair accessories like scrunchies or hairpins that won't snag or pull on your braids.

✨Moisturize your hair✨

Apply a lightweight hair oil to the length of your braids to keep them from becoming too dry.

Avoid products with heavy ingredients like silicone, which can cause buildup.

✨Minimize heat styling✨

Try to avoid using heat styling tools, as the heat can weaken and damage your braids.

If you must use heat, use a heat protectant and keep the temperature low.

✨Touch up your edges✨

If your edges start to look frizzy, you can use a bit of edge control or a water and gel mixture to smooth them down.

✨Avoid excessive moisture✨

Too much water can weaken your braids and lead to frizz and unraveling. Protect your braids from excessive moisture, especially when swimming or in humid environments.

✨Regular maintenance✨

Depending on your hair type and the style, you may need to get your braids redone or tightened after a few weeks to maintain their appearance and protect your natural hair.

✨Listen to your hair and scalp✨

Pay attention to any discomfort, itching, or unusual changes in your scalp. If you experience any issues, consult a professional or remove your braids.
